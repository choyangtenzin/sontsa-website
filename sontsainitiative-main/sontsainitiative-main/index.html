OUR MISSION

To provide Tibetan students in exile free tools and mentorship to ace their undergraduate college application in the US

Outcome:

We hope to promote awareness of the higher education opportunities in the US to Tibetan youth in Exile. We strive to provide tools such as essay brainstorming, editing, SAT/TOEFL, Extracurricular, Interview, and resume advising fully by volunteering mentors with diverse backgrounds. We aim to expand the understanding of education beyond formal schools for Tibetan youths in exile and promote creativity.

Resources:

On our website, we have developed several materials to kickstart your college application, and further resources will come as we launch our initiative.
